Best Commercials from Super Bowl

Mountain Dew - Aubrey Plaza

Mountain Dew brought humor and a dash of fantasy to the Super Bowl with Aubrey Plaza and Nick Offerman riding dragons while enjoying the refreshing Baja Blast.

T-Mobile - John Travolta

John Travolta, along with Zach Braff and Donald Faison, gave a nostalgic twist to a "Grease"-themed ad promoting T-Mobile’s home internet service.

Dunkin' - Ben Affleck and Jennifer Lopez

Ben Affleck hilariously handled a Dunkin’ drive-thru, only to be humorously interrupted by Jennifer Lopez in this fan-favorite ad.
